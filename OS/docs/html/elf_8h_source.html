432 /* 4.7.1 Relocation codes
433  *
434  * S (when used on its own) is the address of the symbol.
435  * A is the addend for the relocation.
436  * P is the address of the place being relocated (derived from r_offset).
437  * Pa is the adjusted address of the place being relocated, defined as (P & 0xFFFFFFFC).
438  * T is 1 if the target symbol S has type STT_FUNC and the symbol addresses a Thumb instruction;
439  * it is 0 otherwise.
440  * B(S) is the addressing origin of the output segment defining the symbol S.
441  * GOT_ORG is the addressing origin of the Global Offset Table
442  * GOT(S) is the address of the GOT entry for the symbol S.
443  */
